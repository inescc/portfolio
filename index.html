Designing Eyes-free Voice-based Interactions for Text Composition and Review

Editing text without any visual feedback is a challenge for users. We explore the patterns of use of eyes-free composition and revision using speech while addressing the limitations of state-of-the-art systems. Lastly, we include recommendations to inform the design of a potential system.

Re-designing the Website to Increase Conversions and Customer Satisfaction

Company websites are most of the times the first contact with clients and prospects. Companies need to make sure that information is accessible and that tone of voice and design are in line with their values.

Developing a Launching Strategy for a New Gym

In this side project, we--a team of designers and consultants--supported a young personal trainer in his enterpreneurial adventure of opening a gym. The team not only ensured that branding, website, social media and blog were ready for the inauguration, but also helped him developing a startup strategy.

Designing a Platform for Students to Exchange Skills

Students cannot always hire professionals when they need help, for instance, building a computer program or studying for a calculus exam. We designed a social network site (SNS) that allows students to exchange their skills for something else: time!

Exploring the Possibilities and Challenges of Information Sharing in a Household Environment

We designed an integrated media surface to bring household members together by helping them organizing their daily tasks and proposing activities and plans.
